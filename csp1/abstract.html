ABSTRACT

Horticulture is the science and art of growing and caring for plants, especially those used for food, decoration, medicine, and landscaping. It includes a wide range of plant types like fruits, vegetables, nuts, herbs, flowers, and ornamental shrubs and trees .This community service project focuses on exploring and understanding the current horticultural practices adopted by local nursery shops and in households through a structured survey in Kodur. The aim is to assess the availability, cultivation techniques, marketing strategies, and challenges faced by nursery owners in promoting plant-based products such as ornamental plants, fruit saplings, vegetables, medicinal herbs, and indoor plants. Through our survey ,we explore nursery shops and local houses to understand current horticultural practices. We aimed to identify availability, techniques, marketing, and challenges in promoting plant-based products like ornamental plants, fruits saplings, herbs and more.
