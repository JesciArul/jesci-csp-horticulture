CONCLUSION

The Horticulture Community Service Project has been a valuable initiative aimed at understanding, promoting, and supporting the practices of sustainable agriculture and home gardening within our community. Through this project, we have interacted with local farmers, gardening enthusiasts, and agricultural officers to gather insights into current horticultural techniques, challenges faced, and areas of potential improvement.


The survey and field visits revealed the significance of horticulture in enhancing food security, increasing income opportunities, and improving environmental sustainability. It also emphasized the need for better awareness, training, and support systems for farmers, particularly in the use of modern irrigation methods, organic farming practices, and post-harvest management


In conclusion, the project has successfully met its objectives by promoting awareness, encouraging community participation, and highlighting the pivotal role horticulture plays in building a greener, healthier, and more self-reliant society. We hope this initiative continues to inspire others to contribute to sustainable agricultural development in their own capacities
