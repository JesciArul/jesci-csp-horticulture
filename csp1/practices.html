Horticultural Practices

1. Propagation


Propagation is the process of multiplying plants by seeds (sexual) or vegetative methods like cuttings, layering, and grafting.

2. Irrigation


Irrigation involves supplying water to crops through methods like drip, sprinkler, or furrow irrigation to support plant growth.

3. Manuring & Fertilization


Adding organic (compost, manure) or inorganic (NPK fertilizers) nutrients to the soil improves plant health and yield.

4. Pest and Disease Management


Controlling pests and diseases through biological, chemical, or integrated approaches ensures healthy plant development.

5. Harvesting


Harvesting is the timely collection of mature crops to maintain quality and market value.
